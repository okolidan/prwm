Our Services

We Offer

Women’s Health Service

We provide attentive, personalized healthcare for women through all phases of life. Our range of services from preventive care to specialized treatment is designed to support your health, comfort, and confidence at every step.

Pediatric Health Care

We’re dedicated to keeping your little ones healthy and thriving. Our pediatric care covers everything from regular check-ups to developmental assessments, all delivered with kindness and expertise to make every visit a positive experience.

Driver’s Medical Assessments

Need a driver’s medical exam? We make the process simple and stress-free. Our healthcare professionals perform thorough assessments to make sure you meet all driving health requirements, helping you stay safe and confident behind the wheel.

Visa Medical Examinations

Before traveling abroad for work or long-term stays, most countries require a certified medical examination to ensure applicants meet public health standards. Our experienced healthcare professionals provide complete, country-specific visa medical assessments with precision, confidentiality, and care.

Clinically Supervised Piercings

At Plains Road West Medical Clinic, we provide safe, sterile, and medically supervised piercings performed by trained healthcare professionals. Using advanced hygiene protocols and precise techniques, we ensure every piercing is both beautiful and health-conscious,giving you peace of mind and lasting results.

Early Gender DNA Testing

The journey to parenthood is filled with excitement and curiosity. Our Baby’s Gender DNA Test provides an early, scientifically backed way to determine your baby’s gender. Performed with care and precision, the test offers dependable results you can trust.

Always Here for You

Open When You Need Us Most

At Plains Road West Medical Clinic, we make healthcare simple and accessible. You don’t always have time to schedule an appointment, and that’s okay. Our doors are open to walk-in patients of all ages, offering prompt, high-quality care from our dedicated medical team. Whether it’s a sudden illness or a quick consultation, you’ll receive attentive, professional service every time. Many of our walk-in visitors appreciate the experience so much that they return to us for their ongoing healthcare needs.
