About PRWM Clinic

Enjoy Quality Health Care With Us

At Plains Road West Medical Clinic, we are dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services to individuals and families throughout Burlington and the surrounding communities. Our mission is to deliver patient-focused, compassionate, and comprehensive medical care that promotes lifelong health and wellness.

Always Here for You

Open When You Need Us Most

At Plains Road West Medical Clinic, we make healthcare simple and accessible. You don’t always have time to schedule an appointment, and that’s okay. Our doors are open to walk-in patients of all ages, offering prompt, high-quality care from our dedicated medical team. Whether it’s a sudden illness or a quick consultation, you’ll receive attentive, professional service every time. Many of our walk-in visitors appreciate the experience so much that they return to us for their ongoing healthcare needs.

Our Lead Doctors

Meet our experienced lead doctors, dedicated to providing compassionate, high-quality care for every patient.

Dr. Nnamdi Peter Anagor

MD, MSc. Cardiology & Stroke (UK), MRCGP (UK), CCFP

Dr. Nnamdi Peter Anagor is a devoted UK trained family physician who is committed to providing comprehensive, patient-centered care to individuals and families throughout all stages of life. His practice emphasizes preventive medicine, chronic disease management, and holistic wellness, integrating evidence-based clinical practices with a compassionate and personalized approach.

Dr. Ighiwiyisi Osa Erimona

MD, MRCGP (UK), FWACP, CCFP

Dr. Ighiwiyisi Osa Erimona is a UK-trained Family Physician with over a decade of diverse clinical experience, committed to providing high-quality, comprehensive, and patient-centered primary care. His broad clinical expertise spans the full spectrum of life, from newborns and children to adults and older adults, positioning him as a trusted first point of contact for a wide range of health concerns.
