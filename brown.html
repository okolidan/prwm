Dr. Nnamdi Peter Anagor

MD, MSc. Cardiology & Stroke (UK), MRCGP (UK), CCFP

Dr. Nnamdi Peter Anagor is a devoted UK trained family physician who is committed to providing comprehensive, patient-centered care to individuals and families throughout all stages of life. His practice emphasizes preventive medicine, chronic disease management, and holistic wellness, integrating evidence-based clinical practices with a compassionate and personalized approach.

Dr. Anagor possesses extensive experience in managing a diverse range of medical conditions, from acute illnesses to complex chronic diseases, which he has acquired through his postgraduate training in family medicine, geriatric care, gynecology, and emergency medicine, as well as through his Master’s degree in cardiology and stroke, from which he graduated with distinction.

In addition to his clinical expertise, Dr. Anagor is passionate about preventive health measures, including immunizations, cancer screenings, and lifestyle counseling, all aimed at assisting patients in achieving long-term well-being. He is actively engaged in enhancing the quality of care through the implementation of standardized protocols (SOPs), patient education initiatives, and collaborative care models that involve nurses, allied health professionals, and specialists, thereby ensuring a holistic continuity of care tailored to the specific expectations of individual patients.

Fluent in English, Dr. Anagor employs a patient-centered approach during consultations, valuing patient’s ideas, beliefs, concerns, and expectations in order to formulate a robust quality of care while respecting their autonomy. He has a special interest in the care of the elderly, as well as women’s and men’s health, although his expertise is not limited to these areas within family medicine.

Beyond his professional commitments, Dr. Anagor is an enthusiastic fan of Formula 1 car racing and enjoys soccer, tennis, mixed martial arts (MMA), and boxing.
