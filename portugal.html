Work Offer

Both skilled and unskilled roles available. Job allocation based on qualifications and 1 month mandatory pre-employment training on arrival.

Available Job Offers;

  • Agriculture Farm Workers (Multiple locations)[Salary: 2,000€/month]
  • Warehouse Workers (Product Packaging, Lisbon)[Salary: 2,200€/month]

Contract Details:

  • Valid Job Offer
  • Portugal Work Permit
  • Accommodation provided by employer
  • Portugal Residence Visa
  • Job Contract and Work Permit processed together
  • Initial Visa issued: 12 months
  • On arrival: Apply for Temporary Residence (2 years, renewable)
  • Average processing time: 16 weeks
  • Schengen Visa for France averages 15 working days (3 weeks)

Costs:

  • Initial Deposit: $900 (₦1,350,000)
  • After Work Permit release: $2,600 (₦3,900,000)
  • Total: $3,500 (~₦5,250,000)
    (Balance covers visa processing, appointment, police clearance, health insurance, documentation)

Documents Required (PDF copies):

  • CV/Resume (with address & phone number)
  • Biodata page of international passport
  • NIN Card/National ID
  • 5 passport photos (white background)
  • WAEC & Degree Certificates
