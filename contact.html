PRWM CLINIC

Plains Road West Medical Clinic is a trusted community healthcare provider dedicated to delivering high-quality, patient-centered medical services for individuals and families. Our clinic offers a wide range of healthcare solutions, including family medicine, walk-in care, women’s health, pediatric services, chronic disease management, driver’s and visa medicals, and specialized services such as medical piercings and early gender DNA testing.
